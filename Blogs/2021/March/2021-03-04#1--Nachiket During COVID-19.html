Nachiket During COVID-19

Asha Pawagi | MARCH 4, 2021

Children we serve totally depend on Nachiket for their educational and reading needs. They are falling behind at alarming rates.


The staff is collaborating with other organizations with similar aims, and Primary School teachers. This way we are able to provide library services to small groups of children across the village and at the library.
