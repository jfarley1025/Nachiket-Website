A Thank You Note to Donors

Asha Pawagi | MARCH 4, 2021

By gift-wrapping the quest for knowledge, joy of reading and inspiration, you are paving the way to a brighter future.


Because of your enthusiastic support we continue to provide and maintain a creative learning forum, a second-to-none book collection, and unique library services such as Storytelling in which mothers are involved. These are the home without books.


Your financial support is invaluable; every donated dollar has a huge impact on a child's life.


Your generous support means we can update learning resources and expand our reach to more villages.
